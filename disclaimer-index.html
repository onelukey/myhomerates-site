Calculator outputs

The mortgage calculator on this site is provided as an educational tool only. Outputs are estimates based on the figures you enter and standard amortisation calculations. They do not account for:

  • Property taxes or homeowners insurance (which are typically included in monthly escrow payments)
  • Private mortgage insurance (PMI) premiums beyond the indicative note shown
  • HOA fees, closing costs, or other transaction costs
  • Your actual credit profile, which will affect the rate a lender offers you
  • Lender-specific fees, points, or terms

Calculator results are not a mortgage quote. Only a licensed lender can provide an official Loan Estimate.
