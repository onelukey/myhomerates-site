Privacy Policy

MyHomeRates.com

Short version: We don't sell your data. We don't build email lists. The only data we collect is anonymous analytics (Google Analytics 4) and the message you voluntarily send via our contact form.

Who we are

MyHomeRates.com is an independent personal finance education website owned and operated by Mike Lucas, based in the United Kingdom. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit this site.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us.

What data we collect and why

Data typeHow collectedWhyRetained by
Anonymous usage data Google Analytics 4 automatically Understand site usage, improve content Google (26 months default)

We do not collect your IP address beyond what is standard in server logs. We do not create user accounts. We do not build or maintain email marketing lists.

Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous data about how visitors use this site — pages visited, time on site, country, device type. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. See Google's Privacy Policy for more.

Google AdSense

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-3723787015797542). AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this and other sites.

  • We do not control which ads appear — Google selects them automatically.
  • We do not receive personal data from Google about individual ad viewers.
  • Opt out at Google's Ad Settings.

Contact form data (Formspree)

When you submit our contact form, your name, email, and message are sent via Formspree, a third-party form processor, to our inbox.

  • Your details are used solely to reply to your message.
  • We do not add you to any mailing list.
  • You can request deletion of your data anytime via contact.

Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics and Google AdSense. We do not set any first-party cookies of our own beyond what is technically necessary for the site to function. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

Your rights (UK and EEA residents)

Under UK/EU GDPR, you have rights including access, correction, or deletion of personal data we hold. As we collect very little personal data:

  • You can request deletion of any contact form message you sent us.
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics and AdSense tracking as described above.
  • Contact us anytime via the contact page to exercise any data right.

We will respond to data requests within 30 days.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data via this site, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of this site constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For any privacy-related question, use our contact page. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
